A Simple yet Powerful Navigation App

With 3 Navigation Modes and a stunning HUD Display

Turn on the HUD and place your device on the dashboard for a truly magical experience.

Search a business

Displays multiple route to the chosen destination with traffic boards to select the route.

Just tap other destination pins to find the route automatically

Road Navigation Mode

Displays the road in 3D with gorgeous perspectives and an assistance screen with a top-level view of the route.

Sign Navigation Mode

Displays the next turn direction sign in the middle of the screen which "fills up" as the turn approaches. The color of the sign changes to Green when the turn is very close.

List Navigation Mode

Displays the detailed instructions of the entire route. The instruction bar "fills up" as the turn approches and vanishes away when the turn is "over". The color of the bar changes to green the turn is near.
